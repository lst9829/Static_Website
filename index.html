Cities I've Lived In

I was born and raised in Shelter Island, NY for most of my life. I spent 18 years there before I went off to college. I lived in East Stroudsburg, PA for a few years and attended East Stroudsburg University until school got too expensive to continue. Then I lived with my wife and her family for a year in Bethlehem, PA for about a year while I was waiting to go to basic training for the Air Force. Finally, I got stationed in Great Falls, MT where I've lived for my entire career.
